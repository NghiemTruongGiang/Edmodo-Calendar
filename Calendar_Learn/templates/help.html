User guide

Introduction

Edmodo Calendar - Team3 provides an effective online calendar

Main function
. See current time, and any date
. Creating timetable
. Manage your timetable, group's works
. Sharing timetable
. Creating timetable for group
. Remind note, mission, assginment...
. Convert between Lunar and Solar date

login

To login, type your username and password in "Username" and "Password" box at homepage Click "Login" button to login and start using your calendar

logout

To logout, click on the Account button at the top right on user page Click logout

user

User can create group, add photos, add friends and use group's calendar. To use user function, click on the Profile button at the user page.

infomation

To see your information, click Information button.

Photo

To see your photos, click on "My photo" button. After that, to add photo, click on "Add photo" button.

Group

To create group, click "Group" button, then click "Create group"

Lunar Solar Convert

Look at Lịch Việt Nam on the bottom right at user page. To convert date between Lunar date and Solar date type the date, month, year on "Ngày", "Tháng", "Năm" box. Click on circle button Duong --> Am to convert Solar date to Lunar date Click on circle button Am --> Duong to convert Lunar date to Solar date Click on "Chuyển đổi" button to convert

Note

To make note . Double click on any date on the calendar . Write your note/ mission/ assignment or anything at that box To modify note . Double click on date which you want to modify . Erase old note and write your new note To delete note . Double click on date which you want to delete . Erase whole the note
